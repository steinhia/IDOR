# Agenesis of the corpus callosum and autism: a comprehensive comparison
 
## Resume 
* Agenesis of the corpus callosum is a congenital condition in which the corpus callosum fails to develop; such individuals exhibit localized deficits in non-literal language comprehension, humour, theory of mind and social reasoning.
* may overlap with the profile of autism spectrum disorders, particularly with respect to impairments in social interaction and communication.
* 8/ 26 (about a third) of agenesis subjects presented with autism.
* support the hypothesis that congenital disruption of the corpus callosum constitutes a major risk factor for developing autism.
