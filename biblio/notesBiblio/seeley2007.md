# Dissociable Intrinsic Connectivity Networks for Salience Processing and Executive Control
 
## Resume 
* Intrinsic Connectivity Networks, ICNs
    * Salience Network (detect saliant stimuli that deserve attention across sensory modalities)
    * Executive Control Network (conscious regulation of cognitive functions)
    * the 2 are distinct and function independantly
 
## Introduction 
* use term intrinsic connectivity network rather than resting-state -> brain never really rests
* salience network (mostly paralimbic structures : interface reason/emotions etc)
    * dorsal anterior cingulate cortex (dACC) 
        * higher level of activation = higher anticipative anxiety
    * orbital fronto-insula 
    * salience network involved in identiﬁcation of novel or relevant stimuli across sensory modalities
    * These regions coactivate in response to varied forms of salience, including the emotional dimensions of pain, empathy for pain, metabolic stress,  hunger, or pleasurable touch, enjoyable “chills” to music, faces of loved ones or allies, and social rejection. 
* executive function network
    * intraparietal sulcus 
        * stronger connectivity = better executive task performance. 
    * stimulus selection
