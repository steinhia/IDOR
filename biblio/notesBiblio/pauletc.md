# Summary of a lot of articles with Paul as collaborator (articles relative to AgCC)

## Haisley 2025 : Adaptive Functioning Development in Infants with Agenesis of the Corpus Callosum

* comparison of adaptative functioning on AgCC to other neurodevelopmental disorders (fragile X, Down, ASD)
* Vineland Adaptive Behavior Scale Interview at 6, 12, 18, and 24 months 
    * Communication 
    * Autonomie (Daily Living Skills) 
    * Socialisation 
    * Compétences motrices (Motor Skills) 
    * Comportement adaptatif global (Adaptive Behavior Composite)
* 29% of children with ACC delayed in at least 1 domain
* Although there is significant variability, on average, ACC compromises communication skills by 6 months, with reduced motor and daily living skills by 12 and 18 months, respectively

## Miller 2024 : Adaptive Behavior in Primary Agenesis of the Corpus Callosum

* self- and informant-ratings on the Adaptive Behavior Assessment System (ABAS-II), on people with AgCC
    * Communication 
    * Compétences sociales 
    * Compétences pratiques (ou vie quotidienne) 
    * Compétences conceptuelles 
    * Comportement adaptatif global (General Adaptive Composite - GAC)
* self-report comparable to controls, family informants rated adaptative ability below norms, particularly in social skills
* suggests poor understanding of their behavior and its consequences

## Brown 2024 : The corpus callosum and creativity revisited 

* critical role of the corpus callosum and hemispheric specialization in creativity.
* four-stage model of creativity (learning, incubation, illumination, refinement)
* in the intact brain, creativity relies on two opposing functions of the corpus callosum:
    * (a) interhemispheric inhibition to facilitate simultaneous and independent activity of uniquely-specialized processing centers during learning and incubation -> diversité de perspectives cognitives
    * (b) interhemispheric facilitation to support the increased bi-hemispheric integration and coordination which produces illumination. -> creative ideas
* split-brain : pas de résolution créative de problèmes complexes
* neurotypiques : corrélation taille et intégrité du corps calleux/performances en créativité verbale
* AgCC : deficit transfert interhemispherique, vitesse traitement réduite et difficulté problèmes complexes

## brown 2024-b : Imaginative elaboration in agenesis of the corpus callosum: topic modeling and perplexity

* Thematic Apperception Test (TAT) to study the capacity for imaginative elaboration and story coherence
  * création d'histoires à partir d'images ambigues
* récits cohérents (organisation sémantique intacte) mais perplexité plus faible (plus conventionnels, moins diversifiés)
  * capacité limitée à envisager des alternatives

## paul 2024 : Social and Communication Development in Infants with Isolated Agenesis of the Corpus Callosum

* infants with AgCC : fewer words at 12 months, fewer gestures at 12, 18 and 24 months
* no difference in emotion regulation or repeated behaviors
* retards précoces en communication et comportement social réciproque

## Su 2023 : Verbal Problem-Solving in Agenesis of the Corpus Callosum: Analysis Using Semantic Similarity

* The Word Context Test (WCT) of Delis-Kaplan Executive Function System
  * inférer le sens d'un mot inventé à partir du contexte
  * semantic similarity to the correct word was significantly lower overall in persons with AgCC than in controls + loer score
  * capacité restreinte à élaborer des hypothèses imaginatives et à intégrer efficacement les informations contextuelles.
  * suggests restricted imagination for possibilities, limiting their problem-solving and inferential capacities

## Paul 2021 : Alexithymia and Somatization in Agenesis of the Corpus Callosum

* self-reported symptoms of alexithymia in adults with agenesis of the corpus callosum (AgCC)
* + difficulty identifying and describing feelings (alexithymia) than controls, greater somatisation (vague physical symptoms without apparent cause) but similar levels of emotional experience and emotional coping
  * impaired emotional interpretation with intact emotional experience  -> impaired cognitive processing of emotions
  * consistent with callosotomy

## Mangum 2021 : Everyday Executive Function and Self-Awareness in Agenesis of the Corpus Callosum

* Behavior Rating Inventory of Executive Function-Adult Version (BRIEF-A)
  * Indice de Régulation Comportementale (Behavioral Regulation Index – BRI) 
    * mesure la capacité à inhiber, moduler les émotions et se contrôler dans l’action. 
  * Indice de Métacognition (Metacognition Index – MI) 
    * évalue la capacité à initier une tâche, planifier, organiser, se souvenir et surveiller son action.
* difficultés dans les fonctions exécutives quotidiennes, notamment en planification, organisation et flexibilité cognitive.
* manque de self awareness de ses difficultés = difficulté métacognitive

## Brown 2021 : Appreciation of Social Norms in Agenesis of the Corpus Callosum

* The Social Norms Questionnaire measures knowledge of norms and judgments of what is appropriate to do in particular contexts
* Results suggest that adults with AgCC have deficient judgment regarding the nuances of appropriate behaviors in social contexts
  * application rigide des normes sans prendre en compte le contexte
  * pas de différence sur la transgression des normes sociales

## Brown 2020 : Attention, Impulsivity, and Vigilance in Agenesis of the Corpus Callosum

* Conners' Continuous Performance Test (CPT) and Stroop Test
* Des difficultés modérées en attention soutenue, en particulier dans le maintien de l'inhibition des réponses et de la vigilance.
* Des scores plus élevés en impulsivité, indiquant une tendance accrue à répondre de manière précipitée ou inappropriée.

## Brown 2019 : The Neuropsychological Syndrome of Agenesis of the Corpus Callosum

* The cores syndrome includes: 
* (1) reduced interhemispheric transfer of sensory-motor information; 
* (2) reduced cognitive processing speed; and 
* (3) deficits in complex reasoning and novel problem-solving.
* These domains do not appear to reflect different neuroanatomical abnormalities, but rather different domains of expression of reduced interhemispheric communication from callosal absence

## Young 2019 : Awareness of Consequences in Agenesis of the Corpus Callosum: Semantic Analysis of Responses

* Anecdotal accounts from families suggest that these cognitive and psychosocial deficiencies affect the ability of these individuals to anticipate the consequences of their decisions and behaviors.
* Awareness of Consequences Scale (AOCS)
  * lower score AOCS
  * nontypical semantic content
  * réponses moins nuancées -> mauvaise intégration des aspects cognitifs et émotionnels
  * difficulties in the more complex scenarios
* CCL : diff to integrate cognitive and emotional info in social situations + diff to anticipate consequences of actions

## Anderson 2017 : Emotional Intelligence in Agenesis of the Corpus Callosum

* impoverished processing of emotions may contribute to social processing deficiencies
* Mayer-Salovey-Caruso Emotional Intelligence Test : emotional intelligence
  * perception, compréhension et régulation des émotions
  * compréhension adéquate des émotions de base
  * difficulté à intégrer les émotions (pour agir de manière appropriée) dans des contextes sociaux nuancés.
  * régulation émotionnelle efficace -> fonction préservée
* the corpus callosum is not essential for experiencing and thinking about basic emotions in a "normal" way, but is necessary for more complex processes involving emotions in the context of social interactions.

## Rehmel 2016

* Test de Gorham + Sous-test des proverbes du D-KEFS
* worst proverb comprehension, in particular in the free response (lack of imagination)

## paul 2016 : Learning and Memory in Individuals with Agenesis of the Corpus Callosum

* Damage to long white matter pathways in the cerebral cortex is known to affect memory capacity. However, the specific contribution of interhemispheric connectivity in memory functioning is only beginning to become understood.
* Wechsler Memory Scale-Third Edition (WMS-III) : verbal and visual memory processing 
  * globally worse visual and auditory memory processing
  * les déficits connus dans le traitement des visages chez les personnes avec AgCC pourraient contribuer à leurs difficultés de rappel des visages
    * HD : reconnaissance des visages et expression émotionnelle
    * HG : détails des visages/traitement plus analytique

## bridgman 2014 : Facial emotion recognition in agenesis of the corpus callosum

* déficit reconnaissance émotion complexe comme peur ou colère
* attention réduite au niveau des yeux
* corrélation entre lemanque d'attention aux yeux et le déficit de reconnaissance des émotions


## Erickson 2014 : Verbal Learning and Memory in Agenesis of the Corpus Callosum

* California Verbal Learning Test-Second Edition (CVLT-II).
* difficulties in initial learning and delayed memory
  * suggests CC facilitates interhemispheric elaboration and encoding of verbal information

## brown 2012 Decision-Making in Individuals with Agenesis of the Corpus Callosum: Expectancy-Valence in the Iowa Gambling Task

* prise de décision et la résolution de problèmes complexes
* Iowa game : decision under uncertainty
  * plus d'attention aux pertes
  * moins cohérents dans leurs choix
  * plus influencés par les essais récents
* ccl : difficulté à former une stratégie cohérente de jeu + utiliser efficacement les informations disponibles pour prendre des décisions avantageuses.

## marco 2012 : Processing Speed Delays Contribute to Executive Function Deficits in Individuals with Agenesis of the Corpus Callosum

* Color-Word Interference Test (CWIT) and Trail Making Test (TMT) from the Delis-Kaplan Executive Function System
* slow performance on executive functions
  * in particular when mental flexibility and cognitive inhibition are needed
* not secondary to cautious strategy (a lot of errors) but to slow processing time in basic operations
