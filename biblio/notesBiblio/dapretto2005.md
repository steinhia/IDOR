## Introduction 
* ASD : deficits in imitation, theory of mind and communication
* Mirror neuron system (MNS) : goal direction actions, or performed by others -> macaque, ventral premotor cortex, zone F5
    * human equivalent : inferior frontal gyrus  (pars opercularis)
* hyp : mechanism to understand other's actions and intentions, along with limbic systems
  * insula : combines different types of information
  * insula interface between MNS and limbic system, to transmit info into emotions ?
* study : abnormal MNS during imitation and observation -> but social behavior ?
 
## Etude
