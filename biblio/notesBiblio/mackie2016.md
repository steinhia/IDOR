## Resume 
* The present study challenged cognitive control in 15 high-functioning adults with ASD and 15 typically developing (TD)
controls using three novel tasks designed to systematically manipulate uncertainty
* Results demonstrated that the ASD group performed less efficiently under sequential and non-sequential information processing, and had reduced cognitive control capacity under time constraints relative to the TD group. 
 
## Introduction 
* Cognitive control (executive functions) refers to the flexible allocation of mental resources in the service of goal- directed behavior, within the context of input that far exceeds the brain's information- processing capacity
* link between cognitive control deficit and behvioral inflexibility ?
* TI : communication of information under uncertainty
    * Cognitive control is a limited-capacity integrative interface between input and response that dynamically facilitates the processing of information
    * ASD : reduced capacity to information transfer -> intact when simple, difficuty only when difficult
    * lacking systematic and explicit quantification and manipulation of uncertainty within-tasks to examine cognitive control in ASD

## Etude 
* 1) efficiency of cognitive control in sequential information processing; 
* 2) cognitive control efficiency across a range of uncertainty values of non-sequential events; and 
* 3) the limits of cognitive control capacity under time constraints

* Entropy variation task (EVT)
    * has to indicate the direction of the arrow, manipulating entropy (always left, 50% etc)
    * ASD moins bons, effet d'H/surprise, pour chaque paire de H -> effet très fort
    * pas d'interaction, effet d'H/surprise similaire pour les 2 groupes
* Majority Function Task (MFT)
  * cross in several directions, has to estimate in which direction the max of arrows, different left/righ quotient
  * ASD moins bons, effet H + interaction -> ASD - affecté par l'entropie, convergence à la fin
* Dual Conflict Task (DCT) : contrainte de traitement ET de temps 
  * 2 tâches successives, parfois instantané, parfois + de temps, une flèche entourée de flèches congruentes ou non -> identifier
  * impact du conflit et de la gestion des ressources en traitement séquentiel et simultané
  * pas d'effet de groupe pour tâche 1 mais pour tâche 2 (haute charge cognitive), effet d'H, interaction significative
* pas de corrélation avec la sévérité des symptomes
 
## Discussion 
* EVT suggests ASD worst and goes lower than TD as diff increases
* MFT suggests ASD worst when easy, converging at more difficult levels
* DCT suggests ASD same when easy, but lowers more rapidly
* contredicts theories that says that results should be the same at low difficulty (Minshew)
  * mais dans EVT/MFT, requires more spatial attention
* conclusion toute nulle, pas de modèle entre les 3 qui fit les 3 tâches...
