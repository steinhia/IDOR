## Introduction 
*  Donaldson2018 : discussion on social deficit should move to preferences suggested reframing the discussion about the friendships of autistic people from a discussion focused on social defcits to a discussion of differences in preferences and practices
  * more what they Want rather than how differently they act
  * prevalence of medical model of disability approaches
  * “treat” autism by teaching autistic people how to behave more like their neurotypical peers
  * goal: rethinking how to support autistic social behaviors and friendship outcomes
* communication more enjoyable and efficient within same neurotype
lack of congruence in the friendship preferences, practices, and friendship norms between ASD/non-ASD
* petrina2016 : congruency in satisfaction in relationship
  * congruency in reciprocity/interests/values
  * incongruencies in some qualities : conflict and betrayal
    * greatest diference in perceptions related to the extent disagreements were resolved
* finke 2019 : ASD want less emotionally and physically close
* friendship norms/expectations (linked to friendship satisfaction)
